Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How long does it take to get a quote after submitting my idea?
We typically provide a detailed proposal within 2-3 business days after reviewing your project requirements. For complex projects, it might take up to 5 business days.
What if I need changes during the development process?
We include revision rounds in all our packages. Minor changes are usually accommodated within the scope. For significant changes, we'll provide an updated quote and timeline.
Do you provide maintenance after the project is completed?
Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages at competitive rates. This includes security updates, bug fixes, and minor enhancements to keep your project running smoothly.
Who owns the intellectual property rights to the completed project?
You retain full ownership of all intellectual property rights to your project. We only ask for permission to showcase the work in our portfolio (which you can opt out of).
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Google Pay. For enterprise projects, we also accept bank transfers.
